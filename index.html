WHO IS MAURICIO RIVERA?

And Why Is He Attacking San Francisco While Gojira and Mothra Stuck In Omphaloskepsis?


SAN FRANCISCO, My name is Mauricio Alexander Rivera and I’m a born and raised San Franciscan with interests in finance, technology, development, user interfaces, and sports.

Some of my favorite things are Pocket, the San Francisco Giants, FC Barça, USMNT, github, the San Francisco 49ers, guitars, Neal Stephenson books. I love Wong Kar Wai and Woody Allen films, Mexican food, The Long Now Foundation, and the Golden State Warriors.

You can read my writing, browse my films list, browse my books list and send me an email. I can also be found on Twitter, Github, and LinkedIn
