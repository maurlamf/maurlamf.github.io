About

Hi my name is Mauricio Rivera and I'm a San Francisco native with interests in finance, technology, development, user interfaces, and sports.

I recently moved back to San Francisco by way of Los Angeles.

I have worked on a number of cool projects. Most recently I worked at GoldSilver.com, a global gold and silver dealership, where I ran technology, user interface, and finance teams. I have done work as a financial and technology consultant, and previously I worked at Loyola Marymount University.

I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with a BA and Loyola Marymount University where I earned an MBA with dual emphases in Finance and Decision Science. I am also a CFA Charterholder.

Some of my favorite things include Pocket, the San Francisco Giants, FC Barça, USMNT, github, the San Francisco 49ers, chess, guitars, and Neal Stephenson books. I love Wong Kar Wai and Woody Allen films, Mexican food, The Long Now Foundation, and the Golden State Warriors.

Colophon

This website was created on a MacBook Air with Google Chrome and Sublime Text 3. The site is published with Jekyll and the film list was built on Tumblr. Most of the pages are composed in Markdown.

The types are set in PT Serif, PT Sans, Monaco (for code blocks), and Helvetic Neue. All are served by Google Web Fonts.
